The Lodge and Cabins

Originally built in 1919 as a fishing lodge, with cabins surrounding the main dining room and kitchen, Sunset Lodge is a place where families have come for generations to fish, swim, play and relax.

The Lodge and surrounding four cabins sit on 510 feet of waterfront. The buildings are all close to the water, offering beautiful views of the lake, far shore, and stunning sunsets. The lodge has a common room, a large kitchen that is well-equipped for catering to large groups, and a long screened porch facing the lake. Each cabin also has its own kitchen, bathroom and screened porch.

A large lawn next to the water is great for games; there is a shuffleboard court, barbeques, a large lobster pot, and a fire pit. Two large docks are ideal for swimming, sailing, and fishing.

The Lodge is 4000 sq. ft. with five bedrooms (all of which face the lake) and four bathrooms. It has an 80 ft. long screened porch that faces the lake, with enough picnic tables to allow a large group of people to eat together. The kitchen was recently remodeled with two refridgerators, two dishwashers, two sinks and a restaurant stove.

There is a large common room and a newly added games room which contains a pool table and ping-pong table.
