Weddings

Sunset Lodge, with open spaces, water views and beautiful sunsets, is the ideal location for an outdoor, rustic, "destination" wedding that will provide beautiful memories for a lifetime. Sunset Lodge can accommodate up to 26 people.

To maximise your privacy and enjoyment, we recommend you also book the adjacent Sunset Cabins (Sunset Lodge and Sunset Cabins were originally a single property). Sunset Cabins can accommodate up to 24 people, meaning up to 50 of your family and friends can stay on-site. We recommend that you book Sunset Cabins for minimally the day before to the day after the wedding ceremony.

For additional information and pricing, please contact us.
