Rates & Availability

Sunset Lodge and the surrounding cabins are available to rent for 5 months out of the year, from June to October.
During our high season (June 17 - Sep. 2), we only accept bookings of all of the units together (other than Judy's Cabin).

  • Rates
  • Availability

The 2017 weekly rates are below. During the high season, we require a 1 week minimum stay.
All rates subject to 9% state sales tax. Security deposits required.

Boats may also be rented separately for $100/week each.
